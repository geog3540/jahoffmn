The Hidden Herd Animal Agriculture in Iowa

Welcome to the homepage for my final project! This project explores the geographic distribution of animal agriculture across the state of Iowa and its relation to cancer rates. The motivation for this project is to demonstrate to Iowan's the scale and spread of animal agriculture across the state, and also investigate relationships between animal agriculture and cancer rates. There is an interactive proportional symbol map demonstrating the distribution of AFOs (animal feeding operations) across the state, a choropleth linked with parallel coordinates plot of cancer rates by zip code, and a self organizing map relating the two.

Cancer Rates - Parallel Coordinates Plot

This map allows for users to investigate different cancer rates by zip code and the relation between them. Cancers investigated were those associated with agriculture.

AFO Locations by Animal Type

This map allows users to view the percent of each animal type by AFO location across the state of Iowa. Users can choose swine, cattle, chicken, or total and view the geographic distribution of AFOs of that animal type as well as their relative size.
